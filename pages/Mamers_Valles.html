Mamers Valles

Mamers Valles is a long, winding canyon in the north of Mars . [1] It covers 1000 km, cutting through the cratered uplands of the Arabia Terra , from the Cerulli Crater to the Deuteronilus Mensae near the edge of Mars' vast northern lowlands . Through its midsection, it averages a width of 25 km and a depth of 1200 meters.
