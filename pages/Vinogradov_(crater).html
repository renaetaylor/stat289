Vinogradov (crater)

Vinogradov is an impact crater in the Margaritifer Sinus quadrangle of Mars , located at 20.2°S°S latitude and 37.7°W°W longitude. It measures 223.5  km in diameter and was named after Alexander Pavlovich Vinogradov , and the name was approved in 1979 by the International Astronomical Union (IAU) Working Group for Planetary System Nomenclature (WGPSN).
