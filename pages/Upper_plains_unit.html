Upper plains unit

The upper plains unit is the remnants of a 50-100 meter thick mantling that has been discovered in the mid-latitudes of the planet Mars . It was first investigated in the Deuteronilus Mensae ( Ismenius Lacus quadrangle ) region, but it occurs in other places as well. The remnants consist of sets of dipping layers in impact craters , in depressions, and along mesas . [1] Sets of dipping layers may be of various sizes and shapes—some look like Aztec pyramids from Central America .
