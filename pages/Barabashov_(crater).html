Barabashov (crater)

Barabashov is an impact crater in the Arcadia quadrangle of Mars in the middle of Mareotis Fossae , located at 47.7°N latitude and 68.8°W longitude. It is 125.6 km in diameter and was named after Nikolai P. Barabashov , and the name was approved in 1973. [1] The crater is located about 650 km SSW of Perepelkin .
