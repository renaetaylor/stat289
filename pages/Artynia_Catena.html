Artynia Catena

Artynia Catena is a feature in the Arcadia quadrangle of Mars, located at 47°58′N 119°40′W ﻿ / ﻿ 47.97°N 119.67°W ﻿ / 47.97; -119.67 . It is 263 km (163 mi) long and was named after a classical albedo feature at 54°N 137°W ﻿ / ﻿ 54°N 137°W ﻿ / 54; -137 [1] The term "Catena" refers to a chain of craters. [2]
