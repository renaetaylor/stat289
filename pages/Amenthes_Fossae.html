Amenthes Fossae

The Amenthes Fossae are a system of troughs in the Amenthes quadrangle of Mars centered at 9.07°N and 102.68°E. They are 850 km across and were named after a classical albedo feature. The classical albedo feature name was based on the Egyptian name for a place where souls of the dead go ( Amenthes or Duat). The name Amenthes Fossae was approved in 1976. [1]
