Icebreaker Life

Icebreaker Life is a Mars mission concept that was proposed for NASA's Discovery Program in 2015. [3] The mission involves a stationary lander that would be a near copy of the successful 2008 Phoenix spacecraft, but would carry an upgraded astrobiology scientific payload, including a drill to sample ice-cemented ground in the northern plains to conduct a search for organic molecules and evidence of current or past life on Mars .
