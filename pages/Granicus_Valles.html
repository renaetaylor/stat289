Granicus Valles

Granicus Valles is a network of valleys in the Amenthes quadrangle of Mars, located at 30° north latitude and 229° west longitude. It is 750 km long and is named after the ancient name for a river in Turkey. [1] It has been identified as an outflow channel . [2]
