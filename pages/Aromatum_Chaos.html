Aromatum Chaos

Aromatum Chaos is a deep depression, in what is considered chaotic terrain . It is the source of the outflow channel Ravi Vallis , and is situated at the eastern end of Xanthe Terra, in the Margaritifer Sinus quadrangle (MC-19) region of Mars , located at 1°05′S 317°00′E ﻿ / ﻿ 1.09°S 317.0°E ﻿ / -1.09; 317.0 . Aromatum Chaos is 91.5 km (56.9 mi) in length, and has an average width of about 30 km (19 mi). [1] [2] [3] [4]
