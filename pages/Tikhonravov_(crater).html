Tikhonravov (crater)

Tikhonravov is a large, eroded crater in the Arabia quadrangle of Mars . It is 386.0 kilometres (240 mi) in diameter and was named after Mikhail Tikhonravov , a Russian rocket scientist. [1] Tikhonravov is believed to have once held a giant lake that drained into the 4500 km long Naktong-Scamander-Mamers lake-chain system. An inflow and outflow channel has been identified. [2] Many craters once contained lakes. [3] [4] [5]
