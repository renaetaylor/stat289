List of Mars-crossing minor planets

A Mars-crossing asteroid ( MCA , also Mars-crosser , MC ) is an asteroid whose orbit crosses that of Mars . The known numbered Mars-crossers are listed here. They include the two numbered Mars trojans 5261 Eureka and (101429) 1998 VF 31 .
