Cerberus (Martian albedo feature)

Cerberus is a large "dark spot" (an albedo feature ) located on Mars and named after the mythical dog Cerberus . The arcuate markings in the upper right are in the Amazonis plains and may be sand drifts. The Elysium volcano, a yellow area north of Cerberus, has several channels radiating from its flanks. The three bright spots, upper left, are volcanoes partially veiled by thin clouds.
