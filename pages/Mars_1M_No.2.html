Mars 1M No.2

Mars 1M No.2 , designated Mars 1960B by NASA analysts and dubbed Marsnik 2 by the Western media, was a spacecraft launched as part of the Soviet Union 's Mars programme , which was lost in a launch failure in 1960. [1] 1M No.2, which was intended to perform a flyby of Mars , was destroyed after its Molniya carrier rocket failed to achieve orbit. [2] [3]
