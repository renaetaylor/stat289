Mars Cube One

Mars Cube One (or MarCO ) is a Mars flyby mission consisting of two nanospacecraft, of the 6U CubeSat format, that was launched on 5 May 2018 alongside NASA 's InSight Mars lander mission. Mars Cube One was a test of new miniaturized communications and navigation technologies. They were able to provide a real-time communications link to Earth for InSight during its entry, descent, and landing (EDL) on 26 November 2018, when InSight was out of line of sight from the Earth. [4] Mars Cube One is the first CubeSat designed to operate beyond Earth orbit; asides from telecommunications, they also tested CubeSats' endurance in deep space.
