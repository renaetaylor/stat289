Timbuktu (crater)

Timbuktu is an old crater on Mars , located in the Margaritifer Sinus quadrangle (MC-19) region at 5.7° S and 37.6° W. It measures approximately 66 kilometers in diameter and was named after the ancient city of Timbuktu in Mali , Africa. [1]
