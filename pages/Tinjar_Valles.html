Tinjar Valles

Tinjar Valles is an ancient outflow channel in the Amenthes quadrangle of Mars, located at 38° north latitude and 235.8° west longitude. It is 425 km long and was named after a modern river in Sarawak , Malaysia . [1] It has been identified as an outflow channel . [2]
