Antoniadi (Martian crater)

Antoniadi is a crater on Syrtis Major Planum in the Syrtis Major quadrangle , Mars , located at 21.5° north latitude and 299.2° west longitude. It is 394 km long and was named after Eugène Michael Antoniadi , a Greek astronomer (1870-1944) who spent most of his life in France. [1]
