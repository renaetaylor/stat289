Northern Light (spacecraft)

Northern Light was a concept mission for a robotic mission to Mars that would consist of a lander and a rover, being studied by a consortium of Canadian universities, companies and organisations. The primary contractor for the spacecraft was Thoth Technology Inc.

Northern Light is an unmanned mission to Mars, headed by the Canadian Space Agency. The mission is scheduled to be launched in 2009. The primary contractor for the spacecraft is Thoth Technology Incorporated, based in Kettleby, Ontario. Northern Light will be Canada's most illustrious accomplishment in space, and will make Canada the third country to land an indigenous spacecraft on Mars.
