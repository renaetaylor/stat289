Mars Orbiter Mission

The Mars Orbiter Mission ( MOM ), also called Mangalyaan ("Mars-craft", from Sanskrit : मंगल mangala , "Mars" and यान yāna , "craft, vehicle"), [9] [10] is a space probe orbiting Mars since 24 September 2014. It was launched on 5 November 2013 by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). [11] [12] [13] [14] It is India 's first interplanetary mission [15] and it made it the fourth space agency to reach Mars, after Roscosmos , NASA , and the European Space Agency . [16] [17] It is the first Asian nation to reach Mars orbit, and the first nation in the world to do so in its first attempt. [18] [19] [20] [21]
