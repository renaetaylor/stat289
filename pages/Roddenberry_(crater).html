Roddenberry (crater)

Roddenberry is a crater on Mars , located at 49°S 4°E to the east of Argyre Planitia in Noachis Terra . It measures approximately 139 kilometers in diameter. The crater's name was taken from Gene Roddenberry , creator of the television series Star Trek , and was formally approved by the IAU in 1994. [1]
