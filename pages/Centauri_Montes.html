Centauri Montes

Centauri Montes is a group of mountains in the Hellas quadrangle of Mars, located at 38°40′S 95°31′E ﻿ / ﻿ 38.67°S 95.52°E ﻿ / -38.67; 95.52 Coordinates : 38°40′S 95°31′E ﻿ / ﻿ 38.67°S 95.52°E ﻿ / -38.67; 95.52 . It is 270 km across and was named after the albedo feature Centauri Lacus . [1]
