Timeline of Mars Science Laboratory

Timeline of Mars Science Laboratory is a timeline of the Mars Science Laboratory mission and its rover, Curiosity . As of November 27, 2018, Curiosity has been on the planet Mars for 2243 sols (2304 total days ; 6 years, 113 days ) since landing on August 6, 2012. (See Current status .)
