Terra Sabaea

Terra Sabaea is a large area on Mars. Its coordinates are 2°N 42°E ﻿ / ﻿ 2°N 42°E ﻿ / 2; 42 Coordinates : 2°N 42°E ﻿ / ﻿ 2°N 42°E ﻿ / 2; 42 and it covers 4,700 kilometres (2,900 mi) at its broadest extent. It was named in 1979 after a classic albedo feature on the planet. Terra Sabaea is fairly large and parts of it are found in five quadrangles: Arabia quadrangle , Syrtis Major quadrangle , Iapygia quadrangle , Ismenius Lacus quadrangle , and Sinus Sabaeus quadrangle .
