Phlegra Montes

Phlegra Montes is a system of eroded Hesperian - Amazonian -aged massifs and knobby terrain in the mid-latitudes of the northern lowlands of Mars , extending northwards from the Elysium Rise towards Vastitas Borealis for nearly 1,400 km (870 mi). The mountain ranges separate the large plains provinces of Utopia Planitia (west) and Amazonis Planitia (east), and were named in the 1970s after a classical albedo feature . The massif terrains are flanked by numerous parallel wrinkle ridges across Phlegra Dorsa .
