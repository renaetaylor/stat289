Simud Valles

Simud Valles is an ancient outflow channel in the Oxia Palus quadrangle of Mars , located at 19.8° N and 37.8° W. It is 945.0 km long and was named for the word for "Mars" in Sumerian. Note: Descriptor term changed to the plural (valles), and coordinates redefined 3/31/2008. [1]
