Poona (crater)

Poona is an impact crater in Chryse Planitia in the Lunae Palus quadrangle of Mars , located at 23.93° N and 52.32° W. It measures 19.87 kilometers in diameter and was named after the city of Pune , Maharashtra , India . [1]
