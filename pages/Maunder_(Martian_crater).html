Maunder (Martian crater)

Maunder is an old, eroded crater on Mars , located in the Noachis quadrangle at 50 South and 358.5 West, and is inside Noachis Terra . It measures 107.5 kilometers in diameter and was named after British astronomer Walter Maunder in 1973. [1]
