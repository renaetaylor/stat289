Burton (crater)

Burton is an impact crater in the Memnonia quadrangle of Mars , located at 14.1°S latitude and 156.4°W longitude, and is inside Terra Sirenum . It is 123.0 km in diameter and was named after Charles E. Burton , and the name was approved in 1973. [1]
