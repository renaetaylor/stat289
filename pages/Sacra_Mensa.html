Sacra Mensa

Sacra Mensa is a mesa in the Lunae Palus quadrangle of Mars , located between the northern and southern channels of the Kasei Valles . [1] Located roughly 66.5° west longitude and 24.6° north latitude, the mesa is a highland between two deep outflow valleys. The mesa is located just west of the Sharonov impact crater.
