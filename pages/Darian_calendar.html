Darian calendar

The Darian calendar is a proposed system of time-keeping designed to serve the needs of any possible future human settlers on the planet Mars . It was created by aerospace engineer , political scientist , and space jurist Thomas Gangale in 1985 and named by him after his son Darius. It was first published in June 1986. [1]
