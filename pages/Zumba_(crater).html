Zumba (crater)

Zumba is a very young crater on Mars , located in the Phoenicis Lacus quadrangle at 28.68 South and 133.18 West. It only measures approximately 3 kilometres (1.9 miles) in diameter and was named after the town of Zumba in Ecuador. The name was adopted by IAU's Working Group for Planetary System Nomenclature in 2006. [1]
