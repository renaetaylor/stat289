Spallanzani (Martian crater)

Spallanzani is a crater on Mars , located in the Hellas quadrangle at 58.3° south latitude and 273.7° west longitude. It measures approximately 72 kilometers in diameter and was named after Italian biologist Lazzaro Spallanzani (1729–1799). The name was adopted by IAU's Working Group for Planetary System Nomenclature in 1973. [1]
