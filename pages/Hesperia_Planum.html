Hesperia Planum

Hesperia Planum is a broad lava plain in the southern highlands of the planet Mars . The plain is notable for its moderate number of impact craters and abundant wrinkle ridges . It is also the location of the ancient volcano Tyrrhena Mons ( Tyrrhena Patera ). The Hesperian time period on Mars is named after Hesperia Planum. [1] [2]
