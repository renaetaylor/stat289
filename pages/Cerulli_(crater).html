Cerulli (crater)

Cerulli Crater is a crater in the Ismenius Lacus quadrangle on Mars with a diameter of 130 km. It is located at 32.5° north latitude and 337.9° west longitude. It lies in the northern hemisphere south of the very large crater Lyot (Martian crater) . [1] It is named after Vicenzo Cerulli , an Italian astronomer (1859–1927). [2]
