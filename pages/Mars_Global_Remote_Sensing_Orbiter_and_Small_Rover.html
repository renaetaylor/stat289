Mars Global Remote Sensing Orbiter and Small Rover

The Mars Global Remote Sensing Orbiter and Small Rover [4] is a planned project by China to deploy a Mars orbiter, rover on Mars . The mission is planned to be launched in July or August 2020 [2] [5] with a Long March 5 heavy lift rocket. [6] [7] [8] Its stated objective is to search for evidence of both current and past life, and assessing the planet's environment. [1] [9]
