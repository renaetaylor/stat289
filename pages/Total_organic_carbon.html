Total organic carbon

Total organic carbon ( TOC ) is the amount of carbon found in an organic compound and is often used as a non-specific indicator of water quality or cleanliness of pharmaceutical manufacturing equipment. TOC may also refer to the amount of organic carbon in soil , or in a geological formation , particularly the source rock for a petroleum play ; 2% is a rough minimum. [1] For marine surface sediments, average TOC content is 0.5% in the deep ocean, and 2% along the eastern margins. [2]
