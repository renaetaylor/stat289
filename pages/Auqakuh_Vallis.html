Auqakuh Vallis

Auqakuh Vallis is an ancient river valley in the Syrtis Major quadrangle on Mars , located at 30.4° north latitude and 299.9° west longitude. It is 312 km long, and was named for the word for 'Mars' in Quechua (Inca). [1]
