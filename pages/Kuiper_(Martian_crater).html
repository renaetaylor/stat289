Kuiper (Martian crater)

Kuiper is an impact crater in the Phaethontis quadrangle of Mars , located at 57.4°S latitude and 157.3°W longitude. It is 87.0 kilometers in diameter and was named after Gerard Peter Kuiper , and the name was approved in 1976. [1]
