Asimov (crater)

Asimov Crater is an impact crater in the Noachis quadrangle of Mars , located at 47.0° S and 355.05° W. It is 84.0 km in diameter and was named after Isaac Asimov (1920–1992), an American biochemist and writer. [1] The name was officially adopted on May 4, 2009. [1] [2]
