Acidalia Colles

Acidalia Colles is a group of hills in the Mare Acidalium quadrangle of Mars, located at 50.9° north latitude and 23.1° west longitude and is predominantly surrounded by Acidalia Planitia . It is about 360 km long and was named after a classical albedo feature name. [1] The term "Colles" is used for small hills or knobs. [2]
