Pasteur (Martian crater)

Pasteur Crater is a crater in the Arabia quadrangle of Mars, located at 19.4° north latitude and 335.5° west longitude. It is approximately 116 kilometres (72 mi) in diameter and was named after renowned French chemist and microbiologist, Louis Pasteur . [1]
