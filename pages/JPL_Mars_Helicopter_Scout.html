JPL Mars Helicopter Scout

The Mars Helicopter Scout ( MHS ) is a planned robotic helicopter that will test the technology to scout interesting targets for study on Mars , and plan the best driving route for future Mars rovers. [1] [2] It is a technology demonstrator that will form the foundation on which more capable helicopters can be developed for aerial exploration of Mars and other planetary targets with atmosphere.
