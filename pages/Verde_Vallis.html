Verde Vallis

Verde Vallis is an ancient river valley in the Sinus Sabaeus quadrangle on Mars . It is found in the Sinus Sabaeus quadrangle at 0.5° south latitude and 330.2° west longitude. It is named after a river in Arizona , USA. [1]
