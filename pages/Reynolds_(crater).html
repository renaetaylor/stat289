Reynolds (crater)

Reynolds is an impact crater on Mars , located in the Mare Australe quadrangle at 75.1°S latitude and 157.9°W longitude and is in the southernmost portion of Terra Sirenum . It measures 97.5 kilometers in diameter and was named after Osborne Reynolds , and the name was approved in 1973, by the International Astronomical Union (IAU) Working Group for Planetary System Nomenclature . [1] Reynolds form a triple crater formation. North of it is a three crater pair.
