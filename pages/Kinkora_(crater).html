Kinkora (crater)

Kinkora is a crater in the Mare Tyrrhenum quadrangle of Mars , located at 25.2° south latitude and 247.2° west longitude. It is 54.3 kilometers in diameter and was named by the IAU 's Working Group for Planetary System Nomenclature (IAU/WGPSN) in 1991, after the town of Kinkora, Prince Edward Island , Canada.
