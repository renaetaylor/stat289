Henry (Martian crater)

Henry Crater is a large crater in the Arabia quadrangle of Mars , located at 10.9° north latitude and 23.3° east longitude. It is 171 kilometres (106 mi) in diameter and was named after the brothers Paul-Pierre Henry and Mathieu-Prosper Henry , both of whom were French telescope makers and astronomers. [1] [2]
