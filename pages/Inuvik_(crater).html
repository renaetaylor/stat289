Inuvik (crater)

Inuvik is an Impact crater located outside the perennial north polar cap on Mars . [1] The crater's diameter is 20.5 kilometres, and it lies at latitude 78.7°N, longitude 28.6°W. In 1988, the IAU 's Working Group for Planetary System Nomenclature (WGPSN) named the crater after the town of Inuvik, Northwest Territories , Canada.
