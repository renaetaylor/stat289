Dao Vallis

Dao Vallis is a valley on Mars that appears to have been carved by water. It runs southwestward into Hellas Planitia from the southern slopes of the volcano Hadriacus Mons , and has been identified as an outflow channel . [1] It and its tributary , Niger Vallis , extend for about 1,200 km (750 mi). [2]
