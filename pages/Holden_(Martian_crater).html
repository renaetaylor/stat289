Holden (Martian crater)

Holden is a 140 km wide crater situated within the Margaritifer Sinus quadrangle (MC-19) region of the planet Mars , located with the southern highlands. It is named after Edward Singleton Holden , an American astronomer, and the founder of the Astronomical Society of the Pacific . [1] It is part of the * Uzboi-Landon-Morava (ULM) system.
