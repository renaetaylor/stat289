Burroughs (crater)

Burroughs is a large crater on Mars at latitude 72.5S / longitude 243.1W, with a diameter of 104.0 kilometres (64.6 mi). It is named after Edgar Rice Burroughs , the American science fiction novelist who wrote a series of fantasy novels set on the planet.
