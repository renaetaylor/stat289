Galaxius Mons

Galaxius Mons is a group of mountains in the Cebrenia quadrangle of Mars, located at 34.76 North and 217.69 West. It is 22 km (13.7 miles) in diameter . The mountain range has a "classical albedo name". [1] The term "Mons" is used for a mountain. [2]
