Earth trojan

An Earth trojan is an asteroid that orbits the Sun in the vicinity of the Earth –Sun Lagrangian points L 4 (leading 60°) or L 5 (trailing 60°), thus having an orbit similar to Earth's. Only one Earth trojan has so far been discovered. The name trojan was first used in 1906 for the Jupiter trojans , the asteroids that were observed near the Lagrangian points of Jupiter 's orbit.
