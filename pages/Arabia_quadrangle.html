Arabia quadrangle

The Arabia quadrangle is one of a series of 30 quadrangle maps of Mars used by the United States Geological Survey (USGS) Astrogeology Research Program . The Arabia quadrangle is also referred to as MC-12 (Mars Chart-12). [1]

The Arabia quadrangle contains part of the classic area of Mars known as Arabia. It lies on the boundary between the young northern plains and the old southern highlands. The Arabia quadrangle covers the area from 315 to 360 west longitude and 0 to 30 north latitude.
