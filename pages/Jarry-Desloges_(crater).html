Jarry-Desloges (crater)

Jarry-Desloges is an impact crater in the Iapygia quadrangle of Mars , located at 9.5°S latitude and 276.3°W longitude. It is 92.0 km in diameter and was named after René Jarry-Desloges , and the name was approved in 1973. [1]
