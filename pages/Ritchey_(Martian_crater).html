Ritchey (Martian crater)

Ritchey is a crater on Mars , located in the Coprates quadrangle at 28.8° South and 51° West and is inside Noachis Terra . It measures 79 kilometers in diameter and was named after George W. Ritchey , an American astronomer (1864–1945). [1] Ritchey lies south of Valles Marineris and north of Argyre Planitia , a large impact crater. [2]
