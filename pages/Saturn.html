Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System , after Jupiter . It is a gas giant with an average radius about nine times that of Earth . [12] [13] It has only one-eighth the average density of Earth, but with its larger volume Saturn is over 95 times more massive. [14] [15] [16] Saturn is named after the Roman god of agriculture ; its astronomical symbol (♄) represents the god's sickle .

Saturn ( IPA : /ˈsatən, -əː(r)n/ ) is the sixth planet from the Sun . It is a gas giant (also known as a Jovian planet, after the planet Jupiter ), the second-largest planet in the solar system after Jupiter . Saturn has a prominent system of rings , consisting mostly of ice particles with a smaller amount of rocky debris and dust . It was named after the Roman god Saturn (the Greek equivalent is Kronos , father of Zeus ). Its symbol is a stylized representation of the god's sickle ( Unicode : ♄). [1] [2]
