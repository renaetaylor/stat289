Iani Chaos

Iani Chaos is a region of chaos terrain at the south end of the outflow channel Ares Vallis , of the Margaritifer Sinus quadrangle (MC-19) region of the planet Mars , centered at approximately ~342째E, 2째S. This is the source region of Ares Vallis. The chaotic terrain is widely believed to have formed via the removal of subsurface water or ice , resulting in flooding at the surface, and the formation of Ares Vallis. Within Iani Chaos, deposited stratigraphically above the chaotic terrain, are smooth, low-slope, intermediate-to-light-toned deposits that are rich in a hydrated mineral that is most likely gypsum as well as hematite .
