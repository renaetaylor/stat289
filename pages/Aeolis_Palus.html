Aeolis Palus

Aeolis Palus is a plain between the northern wall of Gale crater and the northern foothills of Aeolis Mons (Mount Sharp) on Mars . It is located at 4°28′S 137°25′E ﻿ / ﻿ 4.47°S 137.42°E ﻿ / -4.47; 137.42 . [1]
