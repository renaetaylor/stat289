Galaxias Chaos

Galaxias Chaos is an area of broken landscape in the Cebrenia quadrangle of Mars, located at 34.1° N and 213.6° W. It is 234.0 km across and was named after an albedo feature name. [1] ). Galaxias Chaos may be caused by sublimation of an ice-rich deposit. [2]
