Uzboi Vallis

Uzboi Vallis is a valley lying situated within the Margaritifer Sinus quadrangle (MC-19) region on Mars . It is named after the Uzboy dry channel, now in Turkmenistan , which repeatedly served as the main channel of the Amu Darya river. The Uzboi is dotted with seasonal ponds, lakes and brooks.
