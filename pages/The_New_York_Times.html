It is the largest metropolitan newspaper in the United States . Nicknamed the "Gray Lady" for its staid appearance and style, it is often regarded as a national newspaper of record , meaning that it is frequently relied upon as the official and authoritative reference for modern events. [ citation needed ] The newspaper's name is often abbreviated to the Times , but should not be confused with The Times , which is published in London , or the many other publications that also use the shorter designation, like the Los Angeles Times .
